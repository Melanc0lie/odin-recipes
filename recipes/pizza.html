Pizza

Description

-Pizza is a dish of Italian origin consisting of usually round, flat base of leavened wheat-based dough

-Topped with tomatoes, cheese and often various other ingredients

-Baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven

Ingredients

The dough

  • 1 1/2 cups (355 ml) warm water (105° F-115° F)
  • 1 package (2 1/4 teaspoons) active dry yeast
  • 3 3/4 cups (490g) bread flour
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar

The toppings

  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Cornmeal
  • Tomato sauce
  • Firm mozzarella cheese, grated
  • Fresh soft mozzarella cheese, separated into small clumps
  • Fontina cheese, grated
  • Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Feta cheese, crumbled
  • Mushrooms, very thinly sliced if raw, otherwise first sautéed
  • Bell peppers, stems and seeds removed, very thinly sliced
  • Italian pepperoncini, thinly sliced
  • Sliced black olives
  • Chopped fresh basil
  • Baby arugula, tossed in a little olive oil, added as pizza comes out of the oven
  • Pesto
  • Onions, thinly sliced raw or caramelized

Steps

  1. Proof the yeast
  2. Make and knead the pizza dough
  3. Let the dough rise
  4. Preheat the pizza stone
  5. Divide the dough into 2 balls
  6. Prep the toppings:
  7. Flatten the dough ball, and stretch out into a round
  8. Brush the dough top with olive oil
  9. Sprinkle the pizza peel with cornmeal, put flattened dough on top
  10. Spread with tomato sauce and sprinkle with toppings
  11. Slide pizza into the oven
  12. Bake
