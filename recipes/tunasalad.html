Tuna salad

Description

-Salad dish consisting of tuna and mayonnaise

-The tuna used is usually pre-cooked, canned, and packaged in water or oil

- Pickles, celery, relish, and onion[1] are popular ingredients to add

Ingredients

  • Canned Tuna
  • Mayonnaise
  • Celery
  • Red Onion
  • Parsley
  • Dijon Mustard
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Drain your tuna
  2. add the mayonnaise, celery, red onion, parsley, and dijon mustard to the bowl
  3. se a fork to mix everything together, while breaking up any large pieces of tuna
