Chocolate cake

-Chocolate cake is made with chocolate

-The history of chocolate cake goes back to the 17th century

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups all purpose flour, or (plain flour), (8 oz | 227 g)
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon baking soda/li>
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups white granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (180°C)
  2. Lightly grease round cake pans with butter
  3. Sift together flour, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda and salt into a large bowl.
  4. Whisk in sugar, then add eggs, milk, oil and vanilla. Whisk well to combine until lump free, about 30 seconds.
  5. Pour boiling water into batter, mixing well
  6. Pour batter into cake pans and bake for 30-35 minutes
  7. Let cool for 10 minutes
  8. Melt butter, then whisk in cocoa powder
